    About EBNT

    Energy By Nature Technologies (EBNT) is a green company that is run by Michelle Wilkin as CEO, Thomas Quattlebaum as CFO, and Gene Kern as CTO. EBNT works with innovative companies in the renewable power and agriculture industries to bring clean energy and green solutions to communities all over the world.

    OUR MISSION-G.L.O.W
    • GROWING: empowering, and engaging our valued and diverse people in helping to root a culture of creativity with answers to unique challenges and obstacles.
    • LEARNING: as much as we can from divergent sources in our efforts to bring the most advanced green and ecofriendly technologies and engineering to communities globally.
    • OPERATING : with Integrity, efficiency and foresight ensuring that we produce above, beyond and imagined expectations of those we partner with.
    • WORLDWIDE : opportunities and adventures are sought that allow us to answer the needs and green oppositions of the Future Today.

    OUR VISION…

    To help heal the Earth as best we can through our Incredible People, Passion for Excellence. Meaningful and Various Partnerships; and our Devotion to working with Nature’s Energy Resources. We intend to leave a small ecological footprint while implementing large green energy projects worldwide with Technological Advances. Our VISION is for now, so we can have future generations.

    • Always Do the Right Thing.
    • Foremost are our Clients and Partners.
    • A Respect for Diversity and a Commitment to Inclusion.
    • Lead by Example and with Extraordinary Ideas
    • Joyfully Give Back.

    Industries

    Agriculture

    When it comes to solar power for the agricultural sector, look no further than EBNT. Because the sun provides 99.9 percent of the world's energy, it's clear that solar power is an essential renewable resource for the agriculture industry.

    Commercial / Industries Buildings

    Solar energy systems provide major financial advantages to the owners of commercial and industrial buildings. Solar energy has the potential to cut or even eliminate the cost of power, leading to an increase in cash flow and more predictable monthly energy expenses.

    Education

    Energy By Nature Technologies (EBNT) is mindful of the fact that most academic institutions have a small physical footprint and that it is very improbable that these institutions would acquire or lease more land just for solar energy purposes.

    Government
